Questions

Is this really just texting?

Yes. No app, no website login, no account creation. You text our number, we text back with recommendations. If you're planning with friends, we send a voting link — that's the only web part.

How much does it cost?

Handled is free. Standard text message rates from your carrier apply (most people have unlimited texting). We earn a small commission when you book through our links — at no extra cost to you.

Where does this work?

We're live in the San Francisco Bay Area right now. Los Angeles, New York, and more cities are coming soon. If you text us from outside our coverage, we'll let you know and add you to the waitlist.

How do you pick the recommendations?

We combine local venue data with AI curation to find places that match your vibe, budget, and timing. We never recommend chains. We filter for quality. And we write every description with a real opinion — not a Yelp summary.

How do I stop getting texts?

Text STOP at any time. You'll get one confirmation and we'll never text you again. Want to delete everything we know about you? Text DELETE MY DATA and it's gone.

Do you share my phone number?

No. We will never share your phone number or mobile opt-in data with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
